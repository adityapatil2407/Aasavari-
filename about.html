About Us

Aasavari Tea Stall is a family-owned business dedicated to providing high-quality tea products and services. Our journey began in the picturesque tea gardens of Assam, where we cultivated a passion for tea that extends beyond borders.

With a commitment to authenticity, sustainability, and community, we source our teas from reputable growers and estates, ensuring every cup is a celebration of nature's bounty and human craftsmanship.

Our Specialties:

Grass Tea

Discover the refreshing taste of our grass tea, made from hand-picked grass leaves with a unique blend of flavors and health benefits.

Masala Tea

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of our masala tea, carefully crafted with a blend of spices and premium tea leaves for a delightful experience.

Coffee

Experience the bold and invigorating taste of our coffee, sourced from the finest coffee beans and expertly roasted to perfection.

At Aasavari Tea Stall, we believe in sharing the joy of tea and coffee with everyone. Whether you're a seasoned enthusiast or a curious novice, we invite you to join us on a journey of flavor, culture, and discovery.
